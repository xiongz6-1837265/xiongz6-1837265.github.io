This is NCT Fan Site! NCT (Korean: 엔시티) is a South Korean boy group formed by SM Entertainment. Since the group's initial introduction by their native label's founder in January 2016, the group consists of 23 members as of January 2021, ranging from their late-teens to mid-twenties and participating in a total of four different sub-units, including NCT 127, NCT Dream, Way V, and NCT U.

  • NCT U – Members change depending on who fits that concept.

  • NCT 127 – Taeil, Johnny, Taeyong, Yuta, Doyoung, Jaehyun, Winwin, Jungwoo, Mark, and Haechan

  • NCT Dream – Mark, Renjun, Jeno, Haechan, Jaemin, Chenle, Jisung

  • WayV – Kun, Ten, Winwin, Lucas, Xiaojun, Hendery, and Yangyang

  • TBA - Shotaro, Sungchan
